Meet Simon

Who is Simon?

Hello, I am Simon Crystal, A teenaged boy who wants to use his knowledge of technology, software, and hardware to help people across Carrboro and Chapel Hill. Ever since I was little I have loved technology, as I grew up I helped my friends and family with their tech issues, and now I am bringing my skills to everyone in the UNC area.

Why Simon Support?

Ever year technology becomes more and more importent in our lives, now 95% of Americans use the internet. Unfortunatly big tech companies don't make it easy for all of their users, so I am here to help everyone navigate the importent (and scary) world of tech!
